Level 2 — Intelligence Expansion Agent

Automated Lead Enrichment

Turn raw lead lists into qualified, enriched contacts — with verified emails, LinkedIn profiles, company intelligence, and intent signals added automatically.

The Problem

Lead data decays fast and enrichment is manual

  • Outdated contacts with bounced emails and wrong phone numbers waste outreach effort
  • Manual lookups across LinkedIn, Apollo, and company sites eat hours every week
  • No intent signals means reps prioritize leads blindly without knowing who is ready to buy
  • Stale CRM data degrades pipeline accuracy and makes forecasting unreliable
